TAYLOR SWIFT

RED is the fourth studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swit. It was released on October 22, 2012, by Big Machine Records, as the follow-up to her third studio album, Speak Now (2010). It was announced through Swifts ive webchat on August 13, 2012, in which she also revealed the album tile and cover. Four promotional singles were released in the month leading up to the album release, three of which debuted inside the top ten of the US Billboard Hot 100. The album features collaborations with producers and guest artists such as Gary Lightbody of Snow Patrol and Ed Sheeran and sees Swift experimenting with new musical genres. Swift completed The Red Tour in support of the album on June 12, 2014.
